What is Seedlingo?

Seedlingo is a digital language learning tool targeting adults learning to read their native language.

_

A teacher or content developer can prepare tailored units of exercises from Seedlingo's templates to provide skill-adjusted assignments to a fluent listener learning to read the language.

Seedlingo was originally developed as 立爱种字 LiAiZhongzi.comopen in new window from ideas by 立爱教育 Li Ai Educationopen in new window, to help Putonghua speaking adults learn to read simplified Chinese characters.

To try the Android app, download version 1.1.0open in new window or check the release historyopen in new window for a more recent version. Download to your phone, then install. You may have to enable the setting "Install from unknown sources" before your phone will allow the installation.
