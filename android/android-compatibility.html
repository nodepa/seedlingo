Capacitor officially supports Chrome > 60. That's fine IF WebView has been updated or has been replaced by a compatible Chromium build. That may not necessarily be the case for older phones.

Ionic 6 officially supports Android 5.0/minSdkVersion 21, though compatibility beyond opening the app probably requires Chrome > 60.
